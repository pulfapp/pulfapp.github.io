Why it works

Discover the science behind it

Principles the app based on are backed by science, so you can be sure you aren’t wasting your time, but actually improving yourself bit by bit every day. The principles are called “The 5 Second Rule”, Parkinson's law, and the Pareto principle. The foundation behind the rules is simple: whenever you feel an urge to do something, or when you know that you should (or have to) do something, but you’re afraid of it or just don’t feel like doing it, just count backwards from 5 to 1, and physically move. Also, you have to set a deadline, and remember that 80% of the effects come from 20% of the causes.
